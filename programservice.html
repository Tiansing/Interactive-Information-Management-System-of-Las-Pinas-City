Services Offered

Education

Las Piñas believes that providing care and support is an equal and important component of education.

Clean and Green

From being a backwater municipality to becoming one of the thriving cities in the metro, Las Piñas has maintained its stand on protecting the environment even before the “go green” trend.

Health Care: Green Card

Worried about expensive hospital bills? Looking for an affordable health care plan? Residents of Las Piñas need not look far, as they can avail of a free hospitalization package worth Php 30,000, care of the local city government.

Tourism and Cultural

In an effort to prime Las Piñas as a tourism and cultural site to attract local and foreign investors, several beautification projects are being implemented throughout the city.

Water Lily Festival

Water lilies are known to thrive in bodies of water, growing up to a height of 40 inches.

Manpower Training Development and Employment

To ensure that Las Piñas residents are provided with employable skills, manpower training programs have been undertaken by the Aguilar administration since 1995.

Health

Las Piñas prides itself on prioritizing health care, especially during the term of then Mayor Imelda Aguilar.

Urban Poor Renewal and Development

Through the Urban Poor Renewal and Development Program, the Aguilar Administration has been providing decent housing and livelihood opportunities to more than 20,000 families since 1995.

Taxpayers' Lounge

In Las Piñas, you are B.O.S.S. At least when you need to get something done at City Hall.
